# Sprint 9 — More Depth: Member View + Champion Onboarding

Goal: complete both sides of the product — what a champion sees after creating
a group, and what a member sees after joining.

## Deliverables

### 1. Step 3 (group created) — next-steps checklist
- Replace the bare link + one button with a proper celebration + champion checklist
- Visual checklist: Group created ✓ / Add first session / Invite members / Book your own spot
- "Invite members" item marks itself done when the link is copied
- Preview group button (links to smith-whanau as a demo stand-in)

### 2. Member view — `app/my-group/page.tsx`
- What a member sees after joining
- Your group (name, type, city), upcoming session, your personal donation count,
  group total, your badges, quick actions
- Hardcoded to smith-whanau (same demo group as the dashboard)

### 3. JoinSection confirmed state — link to member view
- Add "View my group" button after joining

### 4. NavBar — add My group demo link
- So the member view is discoverable from anywhere in the demo

## Story this tells to a pitch audience
Both personas are served. The champion journey is complete end-to-end.
The member journey is equally polished. The product isn't just a
coordinator tool — it creates value for every person in the group.
