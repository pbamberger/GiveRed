# Sprint 8 — More Depth

Goal: close the loop — show what the product looks like longitudinally (activity over time)
and what the champion's full workflow looks like (including adding sessions).

## Deliverables

### 1. Dashboard: Activity tab (4th tab)
- Mock timeline feed: bookings, donations, milestones, new members, reminders, badges
- Relative time labels ("Today", "Yesterday", "3 days ago", "2 weeks ago")
- Per-event icon and colour coding
- Shows the group is alive and tells a story

### 2. Dashboard: Sessions tab — Add session
- "Add session" button opens a Dialog
- Fields: date, start/end time, venue, spots
- Mock submit: prepends to the sessions list (in-memory, resets on reload)
- Shows the champion workflow end-to-end

### 3. Booking confirmed: group impact panel
- After confirming, show "Your group's total: 48 donations — you're #48!"
- "+1 to your group's tally" moment
- "Go to your dashboard" CTA

## What this demonstrates to a pitch audience
The product has a life beyond the single transaction. Groups accumulate history.
Champions have tools to manage, not just coordinate. Members feel the impact of their
individual action on the group.
